**Re: Architect and Project Management Services — Chen Residence, 14 Wattle Grove, Lindfield**

Thank you for accepting our fee proposal dated 28 April 2026. This letter confirms your engagement of Harrison Clarke Studio Pty Ltd (**HCS**) to provide architect and project management services for the proposed new dwelling at 14 Wattle Grove, Lindfield (**the Project**).

## Appointment

HCS is appointed as **architect and project manager** to act on your behalf in the design, authority approval, consultant coordination, builder procurement, and contract administration phases of the Project. HCS is **not** appointed as Superintendent, Certifier, or builder unless separately agreed in writing.

## Scope of services

Our services comprise:

1. Concept and schematic design through to DA lodgement
2. Design development and construction documentation
3. Consultant briefing, appointment support, and coordination
4. Authority and certifier liaison (BASIX, Ku-ring-gai Council, principal certifier)
5. Head builder procurement — preparation of tender documentation, evaluation of **two to three** invited builders, and a written recommendation to you
6. Contract administration during construction to practical completion, excluding Superintendent functions under the head contract unless later varied
7. Monthly owner progress reporting and milestone advice

**Exclusions** unless separately agreed: interior design beyond joinery schedule coordination; landscape design (we will brief a landscape consultant if required); geotechnical, survey, and specialist reports (we will coordinate appointments); extra tender rounds beyond one formal head-builder tender; legal advice.
