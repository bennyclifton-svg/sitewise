This letter confirms your engagement of Form & Function Studio Pty Ltd (**Form & Function**) to provide architect and project management services for the proposed **commercial office fit-out refurbishment** on Levels 3 and partial Level 4 at 88 Westgate Street, Parramatta (**the Project**).

## Appointment

Form & Function is appointed as **architect and project manager** to act on your behalf through design, authority approval, consultant coordination, landlord liaison, builder procurement, and contract administration. Form & Function is **not** appointed as Superintendent, Certifier, PCA, or builder unless separately agreed in writing.

## Scope of services

Our services comprise:

1. Concept and schematic design through to SSD lodgement (with DA fallback package if required)
2. Design development and construction documentation for tenant fit-out works
3. Consultant briefing and coordination (fire engineering, acoustic, hydraulic, electrical, structural for mezzanine insert)
4. Landlord design review and base-building interface coordination (Westgate Properties / CBRE)
5. Head builder procurement — preparation of tender documentation, evaluation of **two** invited builders, and written recommendation
6. Contract administration during construction to practical completion
7. **Fortnightly** tenant progress reporting during active design and construction phases

**Exclusions** unless separately agreed: furniture supply; IT hardware and licensing; security monitoring contract; landlord base-building works; legal advice; extra tender rounds beyond one formal head-builder tender.
