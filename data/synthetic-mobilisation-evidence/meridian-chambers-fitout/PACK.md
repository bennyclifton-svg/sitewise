# Meridian Chambers commercial fit-out pack

Synthetic evidence for a **commercial office fit-out refurbishment** in an occupied Parramatta tower. Tests landlord–tenant scope split, SSD/DA planning advice, after-hours occupation, fire and acoustic consulting, services-capacity risk, and three builder quotes priced from different commercial-fit-out angles.

**Overlays:** `commercial-fitout`, `architect-pm`, `NSW`

## Three-tranche workflow

| Tranche | Files | Run after indexing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 — Profiling & briefing | `01`–`05` | Create PMP, Create Cost Plan |
| 2 — Fees, brief, risk | `p02-00` … `p02-06` | Update PMP, Update Cost Plan |
| 3 — Builder quotes | `p03-00` … `p03-03` | Tender comparison / evaluation |

The `p02-*` and `p03-*` prefixes preserve the original numbered mobilisation fixture for tests that load only `01`–`05`.

## Deliberate edge cases seeded

- Tenant vs landlord scope boundary (base building, sprinklers, switchboard, slab penetrations).
- Occupied building — after-hours and tenancy-separation cost risk across quotes.
- Services capacity unknown until landlord report and hydraulic review.
- Fire engineering performance solution for open-plan library and egress changes.
- Acoustic separation to adjoining Level 3 tenancy (law firm confidentiality).
- Hazmat in 1980s ceiling tiles — provisional sums vary widely across builders.
- ROM **not** formal budget; budget only from signed brief in Tranche 2.
- GST treatment differs slightly across quotes (ex-GST primary; one quote notes mixed PC handling).
