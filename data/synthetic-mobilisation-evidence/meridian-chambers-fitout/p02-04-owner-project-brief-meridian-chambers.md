# TENANT PROJECT BRIEF — MERIDIAN CHAMBERS FIT-OUT

**Project:** Levels 3 & partial Level 4, 88 Westgate Street, Parramatta NSW 2150
**Tenant:** Meridian Legal Group Pty Ltd
**Date:** 22 April 2026
**Prepared by:** Tenant (reviewed with Form & Function Studio)

## Purpose

Commercial office fit-out refurbishment for 142-person legal practice. Possession **1 November 2026**; occupation **1 July 2027**. Building remains partially occupied on other levels.

## Design objectives

- Secure reception and client suite on Level 3 entry
- Open-plan legal library (~180 m²) with 42 workstations and quiet rooms
- 8 partner offices (3 Level 3, 5 on Level 4 mezzanine insert)
- 6 meeting rooms AV-ready; 2 × 16-person video suites
- Staff breakout and commercial kitchenette (60 seats)
- Secure records room and comms room with landlord riser access
- Premium finishes consistent with **boutique law firm** positioning; no downgrade to spec-build standard

## Budget

**Construction budget confirmed:** working budget ceiling **$1,450,000** (tenant works, ex GST, including builder contingency for after-hours and services interface).

Meridian holds **$120,000** additional owner-side contingency for discovery during strip-out (hazmat, slab edge, landlord delay).

**Landlord contribution (separate):** **$180,000** ex GST toward HVAC plant upgrade per lease incentive deed — not counted inside tenant construction ceiling above.

**Excluded from construction budget:** loose furniture, IT licensing, artwork, landlord base-building works, consultant fees, authority fees.

## Owner-supplied items

- AV package for meeting rooms: allowance **$48,000** (tenant supply, builder install)
- Loose furniture: excluded (separate procurement)
- Artwork: excluded

## Programme aspirations

- SSD lodged September 2026; CC by March 2027
- Fit-out commencement November 2026
- Maximum **28 weeks** on site acceptable if after-hours programme agreed

## Planning assumptions

**SSD primary pathway** per Urban Field Planning advice. **Do not assume CDC.** Landlord after-hours rules per base building schedule (no Sunday works).
