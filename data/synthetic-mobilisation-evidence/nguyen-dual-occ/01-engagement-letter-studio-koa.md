This letter confirms your engagement of Studio Koa Pty Ltd (**Studio Koa**) to provide architect and project management services for the proposed **dual occupancy (containing two dwellings)** at 15 Moore Street, Campsie (**the Project**).

## Appointment

Studio Koa is appointed as **architect and project manager**. Studio Koa is **not** appointed as Superintendent, Certifier, or builder unless separately agreed in writing.

## Scope of services

Our services comprise:

1. Concept and schematic design through to CDC or DA lodgement (as agreed pathway)
2. Design development and construction documentation for two-dwelling development
3. Consultant briefing, appointment support, and coordination
4. Authority and certifier liaison (Canterbury-Bankstown Council, principal certifier, BASIX)
5. Head builder procurement — evaluation of **two invited builders**, one formal tender round, written recommendation
6. Contract administration during construction to practical completion including **staged occupation certificate** coordination where applicable
7. Monthly owner progress reporting

**Exclusions** unless separately agreed: town planning appeal services; geotechnical and survey (coordination only); extra tender rounds; legal advice.
