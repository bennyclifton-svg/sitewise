Thank you for accepting our fee proposal dated 18 February 2026. This letter confirms your engagement of Atelier North Pty Ltd (**Atelier North**) to provide architect and project management services for the proposed **terrace renovation and first-floor addition** at 42 Hargrave Street, Paddington (**the Project**).

## Appointment

Atelier North is appointed as **architect and project manager** to act on your behalf through design, authority approval, consultant coordination, builder procurement, and contract administration. Atelier North is **not** appointed as Superintendent, Certifier, or builder unless separately agreed in writing.

## Scope of services

Our services comprise:

1. Concept and schematic design through to DA lodgement (terrace alteration and addition)
2. Design development and construction documentation for renovation works
3. Consultant briefing, appointment support, and coordination (structural, heritage, hydraulic)
4. Authority and certifier liaison (City of Sydney, principal certifier, BASIX where applicable)
5. Head builder procurement — preparation of tender documentation, evaluation of **two** invited builders, and a written recommendation to you
6. Contract administration during construction to practical completion
7. Fortnightly owner progress reporting during active design and construction phases

**Exclusions** unless separately agreed: full interior design package; landscape design (we will brief a consultant if required); geotechnical and specialist reports (we will coordinate appointments); extra tender rounds beyond one formal head-builder tender; legal advice.
