## Project understanding

Owners propose **rear extension and first-floor addition** over existing footprint (~95 m² addition GFA), internal reconfiguration of ground floor (new kitchen, relocated stair), and upgrade of bathroom/laundry. **Live occupation** on ground floor during construction is a owner requirement where safe and practical. City of Sydney LEP/DCP apply; **heritage streetscape** — terrace contributes to Paddington conservation area, not a standalone heritage item.
