# OWNER PROJECT BRIEF — WALSH HOUSE

**Project:** 42 Hargrave Street, Paddington NSW 2021
**Owners:** David and Emma Walsh
**Date:** 5 March 2026
**Prepared by:** Owners (reviewed with Atelier North)

## Purpose

Renovate and extend the terrace for family of four (children ages 6 and 8). **Remain living on ground floor** during construction if dust/noise can be managed; otherwise 3-month rental fallback budget held.

## Design objectives

- New kitchen and dining opening to **north-facing courtyard** (approx. 4 × 6 m)
- First-floor main bedroom with ensuite + walk-in robe over rear portion
- Second bedroom on first floor (child), existing front bedroom retained
- Relocate internal stair — avoid front parlour loss
- Retain front facade character; contemporary rear addition acceptable

## Budget

**Construction budget confirmed:** working budget ceiling **$920,000** (including contingency for latent conditions and heritage tie-ins). Owners hold **$85,000** additional contingency for discovery during strip-out.

## Programme aspirations

- DA lodged by mid-2026; construction start early 2027
- Maximum **18 months** on site acceptable

## Planning assumptions

Owners accept **DA pathway** — do not assume CDC. Heritage streetscape context understood; prefer quality materials over maximum GFA.
