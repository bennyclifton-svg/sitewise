# HERITAGE DESKTOP ADVICE

**Heritage Insight Pty Ltd**
Consulting Heritage Architects | NSW

**Project:** Walsh House, 42 Hargrave Street Paddington
**Client:** Atelier North for David & Emma Walsh
**Date:** 28 March 2026
**Ref:** HI-2026-088

## Context

Terrace forms part of **Paddington heritage streetscape** (City of Sydney). Not individually listed; contributory building in conservation area.

## Preliminary view

- Front facade and roof form should be retained and repaired rather than replaced
- Rear addition visible from lane — **low visibility** if kept below main ridge and set back 3 m
- DA will require heritage impact statement (HIS) — we can prepare at schematic stage

## Recommendation

Proceed with **DA pathway**; allow 6–8 weeks council assessment. No objection in principle to two-storey rear addition if materials palette respects sandstone and corrugated iron context.
