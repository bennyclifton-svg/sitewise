## Brief

Provide hydraulic engineering and stormwater design support for the terrace renovation and first-floor addition. The design is to preserve the north-facing courtyard as usable outdoor space while coordinating new kitchen, ensuite, bathroom, laundry, roof drainage, and temporary services for live occupation.

## Scope

1. Existing-services desktop review from owner and builder-supplied information.
2. Site inspection to locate visible stormwater, sewer, water, and gas service constraints.
3. Concept stormwater strategy for DA coordination.
4. Hydraulic services drawings for CC/tender issue, including:
   - hot and cold water reticulation;
   - sanitary drainage;
   - stormwater collection and courtyard drainage;
   - roof drainage and downpipe locations;
   - gas alterations if retained;
   - temporary-services notes for staged occupation.
5. Sydney Water Building Plan Approval coordination assistance.
6. One tender addendum response allowance.

## Assumptions

- Existing sewer connection is retained unless Sydney Water requires alteration.
- No basement excavation is proposed.
- Courtyard falls are modest and can be resolved with grated trench drainage and subsoil drainage if required.
- Builder is to price temporary water, power, and waste separation if the owners remain in partial occupation.
