## Background

This proposal follows our desktop advice dated 28 March 2026. The terrace is not individually listed but contributes to the Paddington conservation-area streetscape. The current design intent retains and repairs the front facade and principal roof form, with a contemporary rear addition set back from the main street frontage.

## Scope

1. Review of Atelier North schematic design drawings.
2. Site visit and photographic record of street-facing fabric.
3. Heritage Impact Statement suitable for DA lodgement.
4. Materials and colour review for the rear addition.
5. One round of council RFI response support, up to 4 hours.
6. Attendance at one design coordination meeting.

## Exclusions

- Archival measured drawings.
- Archaeological assessment.
- Conservation-management plan.
- Detailed facade repair specification for construction tender.
- Expert witness or Land and Environment Court work.

## Cost-plan notes

The builder tender should include a clear allowance for front-facade repair and repointing, separate from general external painting. The tender should also require builders to price protection of retained facade and principal rooms during demolition.
