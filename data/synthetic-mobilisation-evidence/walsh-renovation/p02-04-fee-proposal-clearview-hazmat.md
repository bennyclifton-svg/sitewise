## Scope

Clearview will inspect accessible areas of the existing terrace and prepare a pre-demolition hazardous-materials register for builder tender and construction planning.

Included services:

1. Non-invasive site inspection of accessible internal and external areas.
2. Up to 8 material samples for NATA laboratory testing.
3. Register identifying suspected asbestos-containing material, lead-paint risk, and other common renovation hazards.
4. Recommendations for builder tender inclusions and removal allowances.
5. One phone briefing with Atelier North and the owners.

## Tender note

Until the register is complete, builder pricing should carry a provisional sum for asbestos identification, removal, air monitoring, and clearance. The preliminary Walsh evidence indicates an 1890s terrace with later rear lean-to works, so the likelihood of asbestos in later linings, eaves, wet-area sheet, adhesives, or services penetrations should not be ignored.
