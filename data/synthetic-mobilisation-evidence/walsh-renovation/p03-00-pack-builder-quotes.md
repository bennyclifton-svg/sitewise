# Walsh renovation package 03 - builder quote set

**Project:** Walsh House, 42 Hargrave Street, Paddington NSW 2021  
**Purpose:** Synthetic tender comparison fixture for the Tender Comparison Module.  
**Quotes:** 3 builder quotes with deliberately different levels of detail.

## Source documents in this package

| File | Builder | Detail level | Testing intent |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `p03-01-builder-quote-deluca-building-minimal.md` | Deluca Building Pty Ltd | Minimal | Low-looking total, broad inclusions, weak scope certainty, low allowances, explicit exclusions |
| `p03-02-builder-quote-harbour-lane-mid.md` | Harbour Lane Constructions Pty Ltd | Medium | Elemental pricing, visible PC/PS schedule, some gaps and clarifications |
| `p03-03-builder-quote-mason-reed-detailed.md` | Mason & Reed Heritage Builders Pty Ltd | Detailed | Trade breakdown, separate PC/PS schedules, live-occupation pricing, heritage/party-wall allowances |

## Comparison traps to exercise

- The lowest quote is not necessarily comparable because it excludes asbestos removal, facade repair, and meaningful party-wall/occupation allowances.
- The middle quote gives a useful elemental breakdown but still leaves several provisional items broad.
- The detailed quote has the highest stated total but prices the known Walsh risks more explicitly.
- GST treatment, allowances, exclusions, and owner-supplied items differ by builder.
- The module should normalise scope and evidence without ranking or recommending a builder.
