- Temporary propping required for our demolition sequence.

## Exclusions and clarifications

- Consultant fees, council fees, Sydney Water fees and PCA fees are by owner.
- Temporary accommodation for the owners is excluded.
- Loose furniture, loose artwork and window furnishings are excluded.
- No allowance for neighbour-side works unless required by approved design.
- This tender assumes one continuous construction period. Work-by-work weekend shutdowns are excluded.
- No allowance for a full rebuild of the front facade.

## Programme

We estimate 13 months on site after approvals, contract signing, and final construction documentation. Live occupation is feasible during early works and late fit-off, but the stair opening and upper-floor framing period will likely require the owners to decant for 8 to 10 weeks.
