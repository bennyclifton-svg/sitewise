## Inclusions

- One continuous head contract using HIA residential building contract terms.
- Builder coordination of staged occupation, including temporary dust separation and access changes.
- Protection of retained front rooms and street-facing fabric.
- Builder-supplied scaffolding, temporary fencing and amenities.
- BASIX/NCC construction measures shown on tender drawings.
- Installation labour for owner-selected appliances and light fittings, subject to PC allowances.
- Defects liability period of 13 weeks.

## Exclusions

- Owner direct consultant fees and council authority fees.
- PCA fees and final occupation certificate fees.
- Temporary accommodation for the owners.
- Loose furniture, loose artwork, curtains and blinds.
- Major neighbour-side works outside the approved documents.
- Full reconstruction of the front facade unless directed as a variation.

## Programme

Our programme is 41 weeks on site after possession, approvals, and final construction documentation. We recommend the owners decant for the 9-week period covering demolition, stair opening, upper-floor framing, and temporary roof works.

## Tender qualifications

1. Provisional sums will be adjusted up or down on actual cost plus 15% builder margin.
2. PC items will be adjusted to selected supply cost plus 12.5% handling and procurement margin.
3. Price assumes normal weekday hours and no out-of-hours council restrictions.
4. If destructive investigation before contract removes uncertainty, we can convert some provisional sums to fixed prices.
